About Rommax Canada Doors

ROMMAX Canada Doors Inc is door manufacturing business geared towards quality products at affordable price

Steel Entry Doors from the Toronto Manufacturer

ROMMAX Canada Doors Inc's success story begins with it's owner's vision of affordable and quality products. Roman started as a windows & doors installer in 2001 and always felt the need for a quality doors supplier in GTA area. As a result, ROMMAX Canada Doors Inc was born.

Our company is guided by a commitment to integrity, excellence, innovation, partnership, and corporate responsibility.

ROMMAX Canada Doors Inc. also provides professional installation services for our products backed by over 2 decades of professional experience, great reputation and the name you can trust.
